Real Cost of UNL

The University of Nebraska-Lincoln has a lot to offer, but is it really worth the cost?

The University of Nebraska-Lincoln is the state's oldest and largest university chartered in 1869, over the years it has expanded to a total enrollment of 25,260 students as of 2015 and has many top-rated programs.

The University of Nebraska-Liincoln is a member of the Big Ten Conference which is the oldest Division 1 collegiate athletic conference in the USA. Big Ten member institutions are predominatly major flagship research universities that have large financial endowments and are well-regarded academically.

As of fall 2015, UNL was among the top 50 public universities listed in U.S. News and World Report's annual evaluation of America's Best Colleges. Princeton Review and Kiplinger's listed it as a best value public university in 2014 and The National Jurist listed it as #1 Best Value Law School in the U.S. as of 2015.
